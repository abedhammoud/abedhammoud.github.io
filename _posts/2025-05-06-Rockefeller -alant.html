--- layout: post title: "Alant Health Partners with Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute to Advance Neuromodulation and Digital Health Solutions" date: 2025-05-06 background: '/img/bg-alanthealth.jpg' ---

ETOY, SWITZERLAND - May 06, 2025 - Alant Health is pleased to announce a strategic collaboration with the Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute (RNI) at West Virginia University to foster advancements in neuromodulation technologies, digital health platforms, and innovative patient management solutions.

Under this new partnership, Alant Health's CEO, Abed Hammoud, will serve as an advisor to the RNI, providing strategic guidance on technology transfer, business development, and research and clinical trials initiatives. The collaboration will capitalize on RNI's renowned expertise in neuroscience and Alant Health's pioneering capabilities in digital health and neuromodulation.

"This collaboration represents an exciting convergence of technology and neuroscience, setting the stage for breakthroughs in patient care and treatment methodologies," said Dr. Abed Hammoud, CEO of Alant Health. "We look forward to working closely with RNI to drive impactful innovations in healthcare."

Dr. Ali Rezai, Executive Chair of RNI, added, "We are thrilled to partner with Alant Health. Their innovative approach to digital health and neuromodulation will greatly enhance our ability to deliver cutting-edge neuroscience solutions that positively impact patient outcomes."

The agreement, effective April 1, 2025, underscores a mutual dedication to improving healthcare outcomes through groundbreaking research and advanced technological applications.

About Alant Health

Alant Health, headquartered in Switzerland, is dedicated to advancing healthcare through innovative digital health solutions, with a focus on neuromodulation and personalized patient management systems.

About Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute

The Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute at West Virginia University is a leader in neuroscience research, committed to innovation and excellence in clinical care.
