--- layout: post title: "Rhovica Neuroimaging wins CHF 150,000 to develop A catheter that reduces risk in lifesaving neurosurgical procedures." date: 2022-12-20 background: '/img/posts/02.jpg' ---

Rhovica Neuroimaging has been awarded CHF 150,000 by Venture Kick to develop a catheter that reduces risks in lifesaving neurosurgical procedures. This innovative catheter aims to improve patient outcomes by minimizing complications during critical surgeries.

The funding will enable Rhovica Neuroimaging to advance its research and development efforts, bringing this groundbreaking technology closer to market. The catheter is designed to address key challenges in neurosurgery, offering a safer and more effective solution for both patients and healthcare providers.
