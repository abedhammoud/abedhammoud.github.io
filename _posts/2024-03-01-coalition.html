At COALITION ltd, we believe that collaboration is the key to success. We have deep expertise in how to supercharge cohesive cooperation within organizations and across supply chains, particularly in regulation-heavy industries. Our mission is to help businesses like yours achieve their goals through enhanced, strategic collaboration.

Today, I am delighted to announce a new strategic alliance with Alant Health, a leading provider of data-driven strategic and operational services that has gained a strong reputation in the fields of medical imaging, surgical navigation, stereotaxy, neuromodulation, machine learning, digital health and surgical robotics. Together with Alant Health, COALITION now offers an expanded range of services in these fields centred around accelerating the development and commercialization of new technologies.

I look forward to working alongside Dr Abed Hammoud, Founding Member and Managing Director of Alant Health to help speed progress in these specialized and compelling fields. To learn more, please reach out via mat.stratton@coalition.ltd or info@alant.health.
