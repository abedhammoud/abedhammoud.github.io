--- layout: post title: "Alant Health Partners with WVU Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute to Advance Neuromodulation and Digital Health Solutions" date: 2025-04-29 background: '/img/bg-alanthealth.jpg' ---

ETOY, SWITZERLAND - April 29, 2025 - Alant Health is pleased to announce a strategic collaboration with the Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute (RNI) at West Virginia University to foster advancements in neuromodulation technologies, digital health platforms, and innovative patient management solutions.

Under this new partnership, Alant Health's CEO, Abed Hammoud, will serve as an advisor to the RNI, providing strategic guidance on technology transfer, business development, and research and clinical trials initiatives. The collaboration will capitalize on the RNI's renowned expertise in neuroscience and Alant Health's pioneering capabilities in digital health and neuromodulation.

"This collaboration represents an exciting convergence of technology and neuroscience, setting the stage for breakthroughs in patient care and treatment methodologies," said Abed Hammoud, CEO of Alant Health. "We look forward to working closely with the RNI to drive impactful innovations in healthcare."

Ali Rezai, MD, Executive Chair of the RNI, added, "We are thrilled to partner with Alant Health. Their innovative approach to digital health and neuromodulation will greatly enhance our ability to deliver cutting-edge neuroscience solutions that positively impact patient outcomes."

About Alant Health

Alant Health, headquartered in Switzerland, is dedicated to advancing healthcare through innovative digital health solutions, with a focus on neuromodulation and personalized patient management systems. For more information, visit www.alant.health

About Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute

The WVU Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute (RNI) is a comprehensive multidisciplinary patient care, education, and research institute providing neurological and mental healthcare for 300,000 patient visits annually. The 300 physicians and scientists of the RNI improve lives by pioneering advances in neuroscience, brain health, and therapeutics. The RNI team uses the latest technologies with academic, government, and industry partners to make tangible progress to combat public health challenges ranging from addiction to Alzheimer's disease. For more information, visit WVUMedicine.org/RNI.
