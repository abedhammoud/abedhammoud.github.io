MORGANTOWN, WV - April 29, 2025 - Alant Health, a Swiss-based leader in digital health innovation, and the Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute (RNI) at West Virginia University have officially entered into a strategic collaboration aimed at advancing technology transfer, business development, research innovation, and clinical trials in neuromodulation and digital patient management.

This partnership leverages Alant Health's expertise in digital health solutions and neuromodulation technologies with RNI's extensive experience in neuroscience research and clinical innovation. Ali Rezai, MD, executive chair of the RNI, expressed enthusiasm for the collaboration, highlighting the synergy between Alant Health's technology capabilities and RNI's groundbreaking research and development activities.

Abed Hammoud, CEO of Alant Health, will serve as an advisor to the RNI, providing strategic insights to enhance technology integration, facilitate cutting-edge research, and streamline clinical development.

About Alant Health

Alant Health is a Switzerland-based company specializing in innovative digital health technologies focused on neuromodulation and advanced patient management systems. For more information, visit www.alant.health.

About Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute

The WVU Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute (RNI) is a comprehensive multidisciplinary patient care, education, and research institute providing neurological and mental healthcare for 300,000 patient visits annually. The 300 physicians and scientists of the RNI improve lives by pioneering advances in neuroscience, brain health, and therapeutics. The RNI team uses the latest technologies with academic, government, and industry partners to make tangible progress to combat public health challenges ranging from addiction to Alzheimer's disease. For more information, visit WVUMedicine.org/RNI.
